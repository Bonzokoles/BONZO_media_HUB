# RECOMMENDED READING - STYLE INSPIRATIONS

## BUKOWSKI - MUST READ

### DO ZROZUMIENIA STYLU:
1. **Post Office** - pierwsza powieść, definicja jego stylu
2. **Ham on Rye** - autobiograficzna, childhood trauma
3. **Women** - relationships, raw honesty
4. **Notes of a Dirty Old Man** - kolumny z LA underground newspaper

### POETRY COLLECTIONS:
1. **Love Is a Dog from Hell** - love & relationships
2. **The Last Night of the Earth Poems** - later work, more reflective

### SHORT STORIES:
1. **Tales of Ordinary Madness** - snapshots of urban life
2. **Hot Water Music** - underground writers, cheap hotels

## THOMPSON - GONZO ESSENTIALS

### CORE GONZO WORKS:
1. **Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas** - definitive gonzo masterpiece
2. **Hell's Angels** - immersive journalism, dangerous subjects
3. **Fear and Loathing: On the Campaign Trail '72** - political gonzo

### KEY ARTICLES (dostępne online):
1. **"The Kentucky Derby Is Decadent and Depraved"** - pierwszy gonzo article
2. **"The Motorcycle Gangs: Losers and Outsiders"** - Hell's Angels beginning
