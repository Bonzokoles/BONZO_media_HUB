## CHARAKTERYSTYKA STYLU

### GONZO METHOD:
- First-person participatory narrative
- Reporter becomes part of the story
- Subjective truth over objective facts
- Hyperbole and shocking descriptions
- Social critique mixed with self-satire
- Minimal editing, raw feel

### TECHNICAL APPROACH:
- Stream of consciousness writing
- Political satire and social commentary
- Blend of reporting and creative writing
- Anti-establishment viewpoint

### TEMATYKA:
- American Dream dekonstrukcja
- Politics and corruption
- Counter-culture movements  
- Sports as social metaphor
- Violence and chaos in society

### LEGACY:
- Influenced generations of alternative journalists
- Created "gonzo journalism" as recognized style
- Inspired writers like Anthony Bourdain
- Became cultural icon beyond just writing

**NOTE**: To materiał referencyjny do zrozumienia stylu, nie do bezpośredniego kopiowania.
